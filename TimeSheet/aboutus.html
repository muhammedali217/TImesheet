About fixZ

Home About fixZ

fixZ is a market-leading mobile platform that connects consumers with qualified, trained and pre-screened providers of essential services. fixZ allows consumers to request services on-demand from the convenience of their home. All you need is a smart phone to operate our easy to use app. Your request goes to a minimum of 20 qualified providers in your area. You can choose to pay cash at the site or use our portal to make the payment through credit card. fixZ has two options: one for a customer who needs a service and the other for contractors who are looking for work.

OUR STORY

fixZ was founded in 2015. With the 25 years experience our founder Steve Marku has in the construction industry, he has built a huge network of contacts in the industry. But Steve realized over the years that a lot of people struggle to find professional contractors to take care of their daily needs for a plumber, electrician, painter or any other odd jobs around their house. He also realized that there was not many reliable sources to find licensed, qualified and reasonably priced contracting companies and personnel. With Steve's access to a trusted network of handymen he decided to help the society at large in finding pre-screened, qualified and reasonably priced contractors.
